# Yakushima Relax

Discover the relaxing experience of Yakushima as we travel from Yakushima Green Hotel to Hirauchi Kaichu Onsen.

## Yakushima Green Hotel

Yakushima Green Hotel blends comfort and a connection with the island’s nature. Its location allows quick access to nearby trails and natural attractions, offering visitors a convenient and strategic starting point for exploring the region.

Leaving the hotel, you feel Yakushima’s fresh air and the tranquility of its surroundings. The gardens and proximity to the coast provide a relaxing atmosphere, preparing the traveler for a day of exploration and enjoyment of local hot springs.

## Hirauchi Kaichu Onsen

Hirauchi Kaichu Onsen is a unique onsen in Yakushima, located right by the sea. This natural bath has been appreciated for generations for its volcanic hot waters, which offer therapeutic benefits and an impressive scenic setting, highlighted by the harmonious interaction between nature and Japanese culture.

Reaching the onsen is an experience in itself: cycling along trails and vegetation, the traveler feels a connection to the island and anticipates the relaxation that awaits. Ocean views and steam rising from natural pools create a serene and memorable atmosphere.
