# Yakushima Adventure

In this journey through Yakushima we will explore the lush nature from Yakushima Green Hotel to the famous Shiratani Unsuikyo ravine.

{% include video id="kr7xOhKaUBw" provider="youtube" %}

## Yakushima Green Hotel

Yakushima Green Hotel, located on the island of Yakushima, blends modern comfort with the natural charm of the region. This accommodation is a strategic starting point to explore nearby forests and hiking routes, offering a cozy and practical experience for travelers looking to connect with nature.

Upon arrival, the traveler feels surrounded by a green and tranquil environment. The gardens and proximity to the coast allow the day to begin calmly before venturing into forested trails. The sense of immersion in nature starts as soon as you leave the hotel.

## Shiratani Unsuikyo Ravine

Shiratani Unsuikyo is a mossy forest and ravine emblematic of Yakushima. This natural setting has inspired artists and has been recognised for its unique biodiversity, home to endemic flora and fauna species. Its history is linked to environmental conservation and ecoâ€‘tourism.

Walking through the ravine means delving into a mystical world of moss covering rocks and ancient trees. The traveler enjoys the trail surrounded by waterfalls and wooden bridges, feeling that each step connects them with the essence of the millennial forest. Light filtering between the canopy creates a magical and unforgettable atmosphere.
